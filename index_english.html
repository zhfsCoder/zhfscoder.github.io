LIBS Technology

LIBS is the abbreviation of Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy, we can use the technology to analysis what and how much element in material.

Since the advent of LIBS technology , the technology has been recognized as a promising new technology ,bringing a number of innovative applications for analytics. LIBS as a new material identification and quantitative analysis techniques , both for the laboratory to be used in industrial field line detection . Its main features are :

  • Analysis of fast, simple and does not require tedious sample preparation pretreatment process
  • Measuring range, high precision , can be full elemental analysis
  • There is no radioactive contamination
  • Of the test sample size, shape and physical properties is not critical and can be detected in solid, liquid, gaseous material sample

LIBS Applications Technology

Field of the environment: libs can be used to detect water, soil , air, heavy metals, monitoring water, soil , air pollution situation .

Metallurgy: Mainly used in steel-making process : analysis of blast furnace gas , slag analysis , analysis of liquid steel , steel and finished steel filter defect analysis.

Detection field: Geological survey , archeology and other fields.

Other field: It can be used in the fields of art , glass , medical , etc..
